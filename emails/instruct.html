Welcome! Feel free to use Sinasprite as often as you need it and do the activities that work best for you.

We recommend using it at least once a day.

Once we've confirmed successful completion of your survey, we will send your codes.

DOWNLOAD INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Accept the invitation you received to join a Google group or iTunes connect. Download the app through the link. NOTE: iOS users will need to have Testflight installed in order to download the app. Check your spam folder if you don’t see an invite. If Testflight hangs, exit Testflight and see if there a Sinasprite icon on your phone. If there is, try to launch the app. The documentation we reviewed indicates that the app maybe installed fine but Testflight is hanging.
  2. Enter your group and client codes. NOTE: The buttons are a little sticky (we are trying to fix this), so you may have to tap it lightly or at the top of the “Enter" key or hit it a couple of times to continue.

If you have problems with any of the steps, need help, or want to give feedback, please contact us at socks@litesprite.com.

OVERVIEW MAIN COMPONENTS: Here is a more detailed tutorial.
  • Journal: Socks guides you through proven stress management techniques. As you do this regularly, see Sock's shrine grow and unlock a fishing mini-game and collect fish with Socks.
  • Meditation: Here you will learn basic mediation skills. Create paintings with Socks at the end of each session.
  • Oracle: Answering the questions from the Oracle will unlock an item you can use elsewhere in Sinasprite. Whenever the Oracle is flashing it means there are new things you can unlock.
  • Shrine: Here you can see your paintings that you created from meditation and the fish you have caught.
